FAQ


1. What products do you offer?
We offer a wide range of PC peripherals including keyboards, mice, headphones, and more, designed to enhance your computing and gaming experience.

2. What is the warranty period for your products?
All our products come with a comprehensive 1-year warranty, ensuring that you can purchase with confidence and peace of mind.

3. What is the return period for products?
We offer a 7-day return period, allowing you to return products that do not meet your expectations or requirements. We strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase.

4. What is the expected delivery time for orders?
Our delivery process is designed to be swift and efficient. In most cases, orders are delivered within 24 hours of purchase, ensuring that you receive your products in a timely manner.

5. Do you offer international shipping?
At this time, we primarily focus on domestic shipping within Pakistan. However, we are continually evaluating options for international shipping to better serve our global customer base.

6. Are your products compatible with all operating systems?
Our products are designed to be compatible with a wide range of operating systems, including Windows, macOS, and Linux. Compatibility details are provided in the product descriptions for your convenience.

7. Do you offer customization options for keyboards and mice?
We are proud to offer a selection of customizable keyboards and mice. Please refer to the product descriptions for details on available customization options and compatibility.

8. How can I contact customer support for assistance?
Our dedicated customer support team is readily available to assist you with any inquiries or concerns. You can reach us via our support page, and we will be happy to help you.

9. Are your products designed for gaming or general use?
Our product lineup caters to a diverse audience, encompassing both gaming and general computing needs. Whether you're a passionate gamer or a professional user, we have products tailored to your requirements.

10. Can I track my order once it has been dispatched?

Yes, once your order has been dispatched, you will receive tracking information via email or through your account on our website, allowing you to monitor the status of your delivery.
